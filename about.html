Our founding

Our founding at NetNest stems from a vision to revolutionize the intersection of technology and business, led by Mohammed Alsheikh. Drawing from a multicultural upbringing spanning Tuebingen, Germany, and Jordan, Mohammed's commitment to excellence and passion for leveraging technology guided the establishment of NetNest. With a background in software engineering and data science from California Baptist University (CBU), Mohammed envisioned NetNest as a catalyst for transforming dental clinics' operations through innovative technological solutions. Rooted in principles of innovation and adaptability, NetNest is dedicated to empowering dental clinics to thrive in the digital age, streamlining operations and enhancing patient experiences. Our journey is defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a commitment to shaping the future of technology-enabled business solutions.
